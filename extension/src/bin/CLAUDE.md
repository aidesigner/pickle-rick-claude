## Probe vs Measurement Timeout Distinction (R-MJCP-8)

`probeJudgeCliAvailability` is a fail-fast existence check (тЙе5s default via `PICKLE_JUDGE_PROBE_TIMEOUT_MS`, max 60s). Only `kind: 'missing'` (ENOENT-class) short-circuits to `judge_cli_missing`. `kind: 'timeout'` or `kind: 'failed'` fall through to the measurement loop so a slow cold-start does not kill the convergence run.

`measureLlmMetricAttempt` is the correctness-check with 4-attempt retry-with-backoff (10s/30s/60s between attempts). ENOENT here also produces `failureKind: 'cli_missing'`; ETIMEDOUT produces `failureKind: 'timeout'`. The shared `classifyJudgeError` helper is used in both paths тАФ no duplicate ENOENT/ETIMEDOUT branches.
